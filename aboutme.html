Introduction

Hello! I'm Ragab, a data specialist and geophysicist based in Montreal, Quebec, I'm passionate about problem-solving using diverse data sources. I have expertise in acquiring and interpreting data, and recently completed a part-time Diploma in Big Data. This equipped me with strong skills in data analytics, interpretation, and reporting. I'm proficient in using various business intelligence tools to analyze database storage structures, design and create data models, and support data-driven decision-making for organizations.

My Experience

I have worked on several applied geophysics exploration projects to support geotechnical, structural, pavement, bridge deck, utility, and archeological investigations These projects were carried out by the following techniques: Electrical Resistivity Tomography ERT, induced polarization, self-potential SP, Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR), surface waves (MASW and ultrasonic), reflection seismic, refraction seismic, echo sounding, and sidescan sonar.
